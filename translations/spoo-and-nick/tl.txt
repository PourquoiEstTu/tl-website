古く遠い最初の記憶は　灯りを点そうと祈る君の姿
いつか人は君を希望にして　妬み　苦しみ　全て押し付けた

The first of my old and distant memories    Is your figure that hoped to light the lanterns
One day humans made you their hope    Envy    Suffering    They pushed it all on you 
  
幸せだけを求める欲望が　君の心を喰い潰す

Your desire to seek only happiness    Ate at your heart and crushed it 

君に祈りを　この祈りを　
柔らかい風が君の心を慰めるように　
いつも祈るよ　ただ祈るよ　
君が悲しみに暮れないように

A prayer for you    This prayer for you 
May a gentle wind (wash over and) comfort your heart
I'm always praying    merely praying
That no sorrow may overcome you 
(May no sorrow overcome you) (May grief never permeate through you) 
(May you never sink in sorrow)
(May you never spend time in sorrow)

故く荒れ果てた城でひとり 灯りを灯そうと祈り続ける君を
いつか人は偽者にして　「全ては嘘だ」と嘲笑っている

By yourself in an ancient, crumbling castle (in ruins)
   You who keep on wishing to light the lanterns
One day humans will decide you're a fake    They'll ridicule you laughing "All of it was a lie"

幸せだけを求める愚かさに　君の心は枯れ果てる

In the folly of seeking only your happiness    Your heart withered completely

君に祈りを　この祈りを
温かい雨が君の心を潤すように　
いつも祈るよ　ただ祈るよ　
君が喜びで満たされるように

A prayer for you    This prayer for you 
May a pleasant rain wash over your heart 
I'm always praying    merely praying
That you may be filled with joy

懐かしい風景求め　何処か新しい場所へ　
君が独りで泣かないよう　私が連れ出すよ

To some new place you go    Seeking a nostalgic scene
So that you don't weep by yourself    I'll take you there

君に祈りを　この祈りを　
新しい空が君の心を包み込むように　
君に歌を　この歌を　
いつも君だけを思って歌うよ　
いつか届くように

A prayer for you    This prayer for you 
May the new sky envelop your heart 
A song for you    This song your you 
I'll always sing it thinking only of you
So that it may it reach you one day (May it reach you one day)
